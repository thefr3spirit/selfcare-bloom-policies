Account & Data Deletion

You have the right to delete your Selfcare & Bloom account and all associated personal data at any time. This page explains how to do so and what data is affected.

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account directly from within the app — no need to contact support.

1 Open the Selfcare & Bloom app on your device.
2 Tap the Settings tab (bottom navigation bar).
3 Scroll down to the ACCOUNT section.
4 Tap Delete Account.
5 Confirm the deletion. If you signed in with email and password, you will be asked to enter your password to verify your identity.
6 Your account and all data will be permanently deleted immediately.
⚠ This action is permanent and cannot be undone. Once deleted, your account and all associated data cannot be recovered.

What Data Is Deleted

Deleting your account removes the following data permanently:

Data Type Status Notes
Email address Deleted immediately Firebase Authentication record removed
User profile (name, age, gender) Deleted immediately Removed from Firestore and local storage
Stress assessment history Deleted immediately All PSS assessment responses and scores
App activity data (stressors, coping strategies) Deleted immediately Removed from Firestore and local device storage
Push notification token (FCM) Deleted immediately Device token removed from our servers
Anonymised research data (if consented) May be retained Aggregated, non-identifiable data used for academic research may be retained as permitted under your consent agreement. This data cannot be linked back to you.

All personally identifiable data is deleted with no retention period. The only exception is anonymised, aggregate research data as described above, which contains no personal identifiers.

Unable to Access the App?

If you cannot access the app to delete your account (e.g., lost access to your device or account), you can request deletion by contacting us directly:

📧 Email us at

selfcarebloom.app@gmail.com

Include your registered email address in the request. We will process your deletion within 7 business days.
